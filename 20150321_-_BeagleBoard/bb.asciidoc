== BeagleBoard
[options="step"]
* 100% Open Source
* Componentistica
** 512MB DDR3 RAM
** 4GB 8-bit eMMC on-board flash storage
** 3D graphics accelerator
** USB client for power & communications
** USB host
** Ethernet
** HDMI
** 2x 46 pin headers
** 2x PRU 32-bit microcontrollers
* BUS
** one-wire
** I2C
** CANBus
